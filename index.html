War God Arena Rules

  • Each player can fight another player by inviting them to fight each other.
  • Every player can refuse it when they dont want too.
  • PVP can give you 1000 ss if you win.

How To Use Tecnique

As its a text based battle, some convention for using skills are to be followed. 

(tecnique name)(tier)
(ability)
(effect)

To use a pill, Use this format. 

>> (Pill Name)
(Pill Effect)

How The Battle Works

  • First, invite a judge. Usually an admin is invited to be the judge.
  • Before you use tecnique make sure its your turn.
  • Speed Pills is useful for turns. You just need to type "》" when you want to use an specific pill but if your enemy speed pill tier is much higher than you than your enemy will get the first turn.
  • Each tecnique you use, will exhaust your qi depending on what tier tecnique you use and your cultivation.
  • You can use Qi regen pill to make your Qi regenerate.
  • You cannot use the same pills in the same round. Meaning, pill effects can't be stacked.
  • You can sacrificee turn to gain 300 Qi.

Cooldown

Its highly recommended to write down the rules of cooldown somewhere, preferably in a notebook page because you will be needing to remember them a lot during the battle.

  1. Defensive Tecniques will wear off after 5 rounds.
  2. Enhancing Damage can be stacked but after you use an attack tecnique the stacked enhancement will be resetted to 0. CD 10 Rounds.
  3. Immobilize Tecniques will have 3 rounds cooldown.
  4. Distructive tecnique(atk) can only be used 3 times per round.
  5. Support Tecnique will have 5 rounds cooldown.
  6. Defensive Tecnique will wear of after 5 rounds.
  7. Summoning Tecnique will now have a 3 rounds cooldown after use and the summon will wear off after 3 rounds when used or met the condition.

Tecnique Cooldowns

  • Divine Tecnique: cooldown 4 rounds.
  • Star Tecnique: cooldown 7 rounds.
  • Heavenly Star: cooldown 11 rounds.

FAQ

What is Nirvana Qi?

After attaining harmony with heaven and earth and congealing nascant soul, you gain nirvana qi. Its said that a single drop of nirvana qi can melt the water and bend the fire.
